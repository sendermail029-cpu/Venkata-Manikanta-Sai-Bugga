# /public Assets

Place your assets here:

- `resume.pdf`          → Your resume (linked from Contact section)
- `your-photo.jpg`      → Your portrait photo (used in About section)
- `og-image.jpg`        → Open Graph image (1200×630px)
- `favicon.ico`         → Favicon

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts CDN in globals.css.
No local font files needed.
